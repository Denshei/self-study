Examples of Abstraction

Car

Think about a car: when you drive it, you only care about turning it on, accelerating, or braking. You don't need to know how the engine works or how the fuel is sent to the system. The important thing is that you can drive it from one place to another.

ATM

An ATM is simple from the user's perspective. You can withdraw money, check your balance, or make a deposit, but you don't need to know how it connects to the bank or processes the information. You only see the buttons you need to press and the money you receive.

Smartphone

We all use our smartphones to make calls, send messages, or take pictures. But no one needs to understand how the touchscreen works or how mobile data is managed. We only care that we can communicate with others quickly and easily.

Elevator

An elevator is easy to use: you press a button, and it takes you up or down. You don't have to think about the cables, pulleys, or motors that make it work. It just takes you to your desired floor without complications.

Camera

When you take a photo, you just press a button. You don't need to know how the lens adjusts the focus or how the image is processed digitally. All you care about is capturing the moment.

Vending Machine

To get a snack or drink from a vending machine, you insert money and press a button. You don’t have to worry about how the machine recognizes the money or delivers the item. You simply receive what you paid for.
